8x8 DIRECT DRIVE LED MATRIX - HARDWARE

HELVIJS ADAMS


what you will need

1x Arduino UNO
64x LEDs of your choice
8x 100Ω resistors
1x Vero board

Wiring

anodes (+) are connected to each other in one column
cathodes (-) are connected to each other in one row
add 1x 100Ω resistor to each row or column


connecting matrix to arduino

Row1 --> pin 2 (D2)   Column1 --> pin 10 (D10)
Row2 --> pin 3 (D3)   Column2 --> pin 11 (D11)
Row3 --> pin 4 (D4)   Column3 --> pin 12 (D12)
Row4 --> pin 5 (D5)   Column4 --> pin 13 (D13)
Row5 --> pin 6 (D6)   Column5 --> pin 14 (A0)
Row6 --> pin 7 (D7)   Column6 --> pin 15 (A1)
Row7 --> pin 8 (D8)   Column7 --> pin 16 (A2)
Row8 --> pin 9 (D9)   Column8 --> pin 17 (A3)
